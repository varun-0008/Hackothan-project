Kharif Season

Monsoon crops sown at the beginning of the rains (June-July). Major crops include Rice, Maize, and Cotton. They require warm, wet weather.

Rabi Season

Winter crops sown in October-December. They require cool weather for growth. Major crops include Wheat, Barley, and Mustard.

Zaid Season

Summer crops grown between Rabi and Kharif seasons (March-June). These are short-duration crops like Watermelon, Cucumber, and leafy vegetables.

Fruit Irrigation

Drip irrigation is highly efficient for fruit orchards, delivering water directly to the root zone. This conserves water and minimizes weed growth.
